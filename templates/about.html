1 Million Bedrooms

Bedroom Bio invites New Yorkers to participate in a collective storytelling experience through the pictures of spaces that are most personal to us. There are 8 million people in NYC and we want to collect 1 million pictures of the most personal space- the bedroom. Share yours and be a part of the intimate story that makes up NYC.

Bedroom Bio a storytelling project started by three graduate students at NYU's Interactive Telecommunications Program. Our shared interest of vulnerability, private spaces, and collective storytelling drove us to create a platform that shares and explores intimacy.

We do not share your info. Ever.
